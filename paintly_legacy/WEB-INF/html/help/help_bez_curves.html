Bezier Curves
A Bezier curve is defined by four control points. Several Bezier curves can be connected to each other to produce a single curve. To draw a Bezier curve object, perform the following steps:

1. From the Tools toolbar, choose the Bezier tool.


2. In the DrawSpace area, position the pointer as required, press and hold the left button of your mouse and drag it in the desired direction;


3. Release the button.

4. Position the pointer over the place where you want to place another control point, and drag the pointer, holding the left button of your mouse pressed.


5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until the desired curve is completed.


6. To further modify the curve, drag the two last control points or nodes.

7. To exit the Bezier-drawing move, press ESC.
