Downloading Image to the Desktop
LiThA-Paint allows you to save documents on your local computer (click here if you're looking for information about exporting your drawings into 3rd party software). While in development, LiThA-Paint uses XML-based proprietary file format. Before attempting to download your image you should save it in your remote home directory. To save your drawing on local computer:

1. Click Open button on Upper toolbar



2. Pick filename of the document you wish to open from list and click "Download".



3. Choose "Save" in download window of your browser.
