Importing Image from URL and from the Desktop
You can upload image file in JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP formats to LiThA-Paint and use it as another layer for your drawings (e.g. background). It could be file on your computer or on remote web-server. To do so, follow these steps:

1. From the Upper toolbar, choose the Import tool.



2. Specify file on your computer in by clicking "Browse..." button or click "Upload from URL" radiobutton and provide path to image on the Internet (e.g. http://www.litha-paint.com/img/litha/logo.gif) in corresponding field.



3. Press "Upload" button.

4. The image will appear in drawing area. You can now move or resize your uloaded image, bring it to top/back and combine with your drawings the way you like it!



5. Certainly, you can upload as many images as you want by repeating steps 1-3.
