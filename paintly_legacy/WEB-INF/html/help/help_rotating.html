Rotating
Objects can be rotated around a given point.

To rotate an object:


1. Left-click on the border of the object.


2. Position the pointer over the rotation center, press the left mouse button and drag the rotation center into the desired place.


3. Position the pointer over the curved arrow, press and hold the left mouse button, and drag the object into the desired position.


4. Release the left mouse button.


5. Click on the rotation center to set it into the exact center of the shape.



The position of the object is now modified.
