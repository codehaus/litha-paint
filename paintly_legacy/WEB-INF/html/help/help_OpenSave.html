Open/Save Document in your Remote Home Directory
LiThA-Paint allows you to store all your files in your personal home directory, so you'll be able to access and edit your documents from everywhere. To save your work in your home directory just follow these easy steps (open document help):

1. Click "Save" button on the Upper toolbar.



2. Type desirable filename in corresponding textfield ("Save document (save to the server)" section). Please keep in mind that only alphanumeric characters and "_", "-", "!", "." symbols are allowed in filenames.



3. When you done, press "Save" button.

To open document from server:

1. Click "Open" button on the Upper toolbar.



2. Pick filename of the document you wish to open from list and click "Open".



Please remember that while LiThA-Paint is still in under development, it uses cookie-based system to detect your files in remote directory, meaning that for now you can not access files you created from other web-browser/computer. That will be fixed in future with implementation of authentication scheme.
