Typing Text
With LiThA-Paint you can add texts to your drawings, manipulate with font size, typeface, postition etc. To insert text to your document follow these simple steps:



1. From the Tools toolbar, choose the Text tool.
2. Click within drawing area and move mouse while holding button to markup rectangle area when your text label shoul be. After that, release mouse button.



3. Type a text and click outside rectangle to confirm


4. After that you can change font options using appropriate buttons/lists on Upper toolbar while having text area selected or move it with Tools toolbar ()
